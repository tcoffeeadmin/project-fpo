What is a funeral plan?

A pre-paid funeral plan allows you to take all of the worry - both emotional and financial - away from your loved ones during a very daunting and stressful time.

You get to plan all of your preferred funeral arrangements in advance, and then pay for your personal plan at today's prices, avoiding rapidly rising funeral costs.

When should you get a funeral plan?

Since 1997, the average cost of a funeral has risen by over 225% †, from £1230 to £4000. That's considerably more than the rate of inflation and there is no reason to suppose that this trend will change.

A pre-paid funeral plan freezes the cost of your funeral at today's prices.

With conservative estimates suggesting that average costs could rise to £8000 by 2024, there has never been a better time than now to get a funeral plan.
