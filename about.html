about us

We work in partnership with Safe Hands, the UK's most recognised and affordable pre-paid funeral plan providers. Our funeral plans are there to help minimise the stress to your loved ones and are tailored to your individual needs. You can be confident that the cost of your funeral will be covered, and your plans carried out as requested.

When you speak to one of our funeral plan experts, they will help you choose the plan that is right for you and your budget.

guarantee

Because all of our plans are available to anyone over 18, we never discriminate against anyone on the grounds of:

  • age
  • medical history
  • health

You are guaranteed acceptance for one of our funeral plans.

The money you pay towards your funeral plan is held in a ring fenced trust fund and the cost of the funeral director's fees and services will be covered forever -GUARANTEED!
